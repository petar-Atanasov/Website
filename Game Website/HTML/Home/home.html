Introduction

Get ready for a high-speed
adventure in Ice Run,
a web game that combines the popular
Fireboy and Watergirl series with
the thrilling action of the Sonic Game.
In this game, you'll take on the role
of different character with unique skill
and power as you navigate through levels,
collecting Chaos Coins and save the day.
Whether you're a fan of Fireboy and Watergirl or Sonic's speedy runs.
